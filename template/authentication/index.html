Albi's Analytics Report

Our Objevtive here, is to create a strategy report to retain Albi's customer base and increase it.

This strategies that we have created, have invovled various schemes and offers that the store can put out, which will help them meet the decided objectives. Our report not only covers the schemes/offers on particular products, but also at what time they must be put, to meet the objectives.

For this we have analysied the data of Albi's international retail departmental stores across the world. The data we perfomed our analysis for are of period length of 1 year, starting from December of 2010 to December of 2011. The data we collected involves 5 main attributes, Product name, Quantity Bought, Date of Purchase, Unit Price and Customer ID. This factors were used by us to create multiple graphs and strategies to attain our objective.
